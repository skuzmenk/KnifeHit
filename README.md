Knife Hit:
Hit the rotating target with knifes and defeat bosses to collect scores and unlock new weapons.

Key Features
Dynamic Gameplay: Endless levels with increasing rotation speed and complexity.

Boss Fights: Every 5th level features a Boss with unique visuals and unpredictable rotation patterns.

Shop System: Earn scores during gameplay to purchase and equip new skins.

Economy: 1 point per hit on normal levels, 2 points per hit on Boss levels.

Save System: High scores, total scores, and unlocked items are saved via PlayerPrefs.

Also it has main menu, leaderboard screen, gameover screen and shop.
The game has specific sounds for menu, for game, for shop and for knifes.

Platform: Android and Windows.
